### 🔭 Currently Building

**[Bharat Pulse](https://github.com/insightsbytanika/bharat-pulse)** · `🚧 in progress`
End-to-end NLP platform on self-collected Indian social/news data (1000+ posts, not Kaggle). Comparing VADER vs. Logistic Regression + TF-IDF baselines, tracking sentiment over time, and adding a RAG layer (ChromaDB + Sentence-Transformers + Gemini API) so you can ask natural-language questions over the dataset. Deploying live on Streamlit Cloud.
`Python` `Pandas` `scikit-learn` `NLTK` `ChromaDB` `Sentence-Transformers` `Streamlit`

**[AlphaLens](https://github.com/insightsbytanika/alphalens)** · `🚧 in progress`
Financial NLP pipeline on NSE-listed company earnings calls — speaker diarisation (CEO/CFO/analyst), FinBERT sentiment scoring, hedging-language detection, and backtesting signals against historical price data.
`FinBERT` `Whisper` `pyannote.audio` `pandas` `scipy` `AWS`
